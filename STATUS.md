## v0.1 Gate

TESSARYN v0.1 is not reached until a real bounded place is captured in three
sessions, privacy-reviewed, independently reconstructed, verified offline,
measured against the reference budgets, and reproduced on a second machine.
The synthetic Origin is deliberately labeled `experimental-synthetic` in both
the fixture and interface.
